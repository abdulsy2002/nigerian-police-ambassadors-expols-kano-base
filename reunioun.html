RE-UNIOUN/AGM

the re-unioun/AGM is a formal meeting held once ever year by the nigerian police ambassadors for members who has not seen others for long time, its a moment of sharing joy happiness and meeting with olders school mates,teachers that you have not meet for long time

purpose of re union

  • to reconnect
  • share memories
  • to catch up
  • discuss progress and report
  • elect leaders(if needed)
  • plan for future
  • make important discussion

activities in reunioun

  • speach from invited guest
  • taking group pictures
  • sharing strories and memories
  • eating together
  • games and entertaiment
  • award or recognition moments
