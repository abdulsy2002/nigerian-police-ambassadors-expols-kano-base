REUNION / AGM

The Reunion/AGM is a formal meeting held once every year by the Nigerian Police Ambassadors for ex-students to come together, remember old times, and reconnect.

Purpose of the Reunion

The purpose of the Reunion/AGM is to bring former students together to reconnect, remember old times, share life experiences, and strengthen relationships with classmates and the school.

Activities in the Reunion

  • Speech from invited guests
  • Discussion of school issues and alumni matters
  • Fundraising and donations
  • Election of new executives (if needed)
  • Group photograph
  • Sharing stories and memories
  • Refreshments
  • Games and entertainment
  • Award presentation
