who are the nigerian police Ambassadors

Nigerian Police Ambassadors: These are individuals (often students or youths) who: Represent and promote the values of the police (discipline, lawfulness, security awareness) Help create good relationships between the police and the community Educate others about safety, crime prevention, and civic responsibility Participate in programs like awareness campaigns, school outreach, and community service

are they actual police officers ?

They are not actual police officers, but they act as role models and representatives.

who are they now

Now they are former members (ex-members) but still identified with that background. or A group of individuals who serve as ambassadors for the Nigerian Police and were previously members of police-related student programs under the force education unit.
