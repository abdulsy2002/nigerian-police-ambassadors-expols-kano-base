Who We Are

The Nigerian Police Ambassadors EX-POLS KANO Base is a prestigious alumni association dedicated to fostering unity, professional development, and community service among former students of Police Secondary Schools.

We bring together distinguished alumni from various Police Secondary Schools across Nigeria, creating a powerful network of professionals committed to excellence in service to the nation.

Our mission is to maintain the values instilled in us during our formative years at Police Secondary Schools while contributing meaningfully to national development and community transformation.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant network of Police Secondary School alumni that promotes professional excellence, fosters lifelong friendships, and contributes to national development through community service and mentorship programs.

Our Vision

To be the leading alumni association in Nigeria, recognized for producing distinguished professionals who exemplify integrity, leadership, and service to humanity while maintaining the proud legacy of Police Secondary Schools.

What We Do

Empowering alumni and communities through various initiatives and programs

Alumni Networking

Connecting alumni across professions and locations for mentorship, collaboration, and career advancement opportunities.

Community Service

Organizing outreach programs, educational support, and development projects in underserved communities.

Mentorship Programs

Guiding current students and young alumni through career counseling, skill development, and professional guidance.

Scholarship Support

Providing educational assistance and scholarships to deserving students from Police Secondary Schools.

Career Development

Offering workshops, training sessions, and job placement assistance to enhance professional growth.

Annual Reunions

Hosting yearly gatherings to strengthen bonds, celebrate achievements, and plan future initiatives.
