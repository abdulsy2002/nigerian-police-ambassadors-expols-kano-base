Introduction

Who Are the Nigerian Police Ambassadors (Ex-Pols Kano Base)?

The Nigerian Police Ambassadors Ex-Pols Kano Base was created in 2022 by Amb. Kabir Sulaiman, the first president of the organisation.

It consists of all Ex-Pols Kano Base members who attended different Police Secondary Schools nationwide. The organisation currently has over 1,000 members.

The organisation holds its Annual General Meeting (AGM) and reunion every year, where members come together to discuss the future of the organisation, including leadership, support activities, events, and school and community outreach.

Our Mission

To serve as a bridge between the Nigerian Police Force and the communities they serve by promoting unity, trust, and cooperation through effective communication, dissemination of vital information, and active community engagement to foster peace and positive relationships.

Our Vision

To build a strong network of alumni as ambassadors and former Police Secondary School students, promoting unity, trust, and cooperation through effective communication and collaboration for a harmonious and productive relationship among Ex-Pols.

Achievements

  • Mentoring and guidance for younger students
  • Preservation of school history and legacy
  • Better cooperation between alumni and school management
  • Stronger friendship and unity among former students
  • Improved networking and career opportunities
