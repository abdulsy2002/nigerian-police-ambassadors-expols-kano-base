WHO ARE THE NIGERIAN POLICE AMBASSADORS EX-POLS KANO BASE ?

The nigerian police ambassadors ex-pols kano based was created in 2022 by Amb.kabir sulaiman who is the first president of the organisation . it consist of all ex-pols kano base members who attended different police schools nation wide it consist of over 1000 members and the organisation hold its annually AGM/REUNIOUN yearly where all it members come together and decide on the future of the organisation,e.g leadership,support activities ,events ,school and community outreach e.t.c

our mission

to be a bridge between the nigerian police force and members of the community they serve, with a view to promoting unity, trust and co-operation through effective communication,dissementation of vital information,and active observation to foster peace, where the nigerian police and the communities thet serve are committed to fostering positive impact in working together in trust and unity .
